ELIFCODE

WHAT’S THE ELIFCODE?

ELIFCODE is a game to improve your coding efficiency by solving short programming problems with other people, at the same time. Players share their solution at the end of a game to help other players improve their coding skills. Players can also participate in regular contests and gain experience.

WHAT'S THE PURPOSE?

⭐ Improve your problem-solving skills
⭐ Learn from other people’s code
⭐ Practice coding interview questions
